Omlette Recipe

A quick, fluffy egg dish cooked in a pan.

Can be eaten plain or with simple fillings.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • Two teaspoons of oil or buter

Optional additions: chopped onions, tomatoes, green chillies, cheese, coriander.

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Whisk the eggs until well mixed
  4. Heat a pan on medium heat. Add oil or butter.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  6. Cook until the bottom sets.
  7. Fold gently and cook for a few more seconds.
  8. Serve hot.
