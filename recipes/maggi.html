Maggi Recipe

A quick instant noodle dish cooked by boiling noodles with the included tastemaker.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1Â½ cups water
  • Maggi tastemaker(comes inside the packet)

Steps

  1. Heat a pan and pour in the water.
  2. Bring the water to a boil.
  3. Add the noodle cake.
  4. Add the tastemaker.
  5. Cook on medium heat for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. When the noodles soften and water reduces to your preferred consistency, turn off the heat.
  7. Serve hot.
