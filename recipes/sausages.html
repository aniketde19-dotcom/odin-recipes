Sausages Recipe

Quick-cooking, ready-to-eat meat cylinders that you heat in a pan until browned

Ingredients

  • 4–6 sausages (any type)
  • 1–2 teaspoons oil or butter

Optional additions: ketchup, mustard, chilli flakes.

Steps

  1. Heat a pan on medium flame.
  2. Add a little oil or butter.
  3. Place the sausages in the pan.
  4. Cook for 5–7 minutes, turning them occasionally so all sides brown evenly.
  5. Once heated through and lightly crispy, turn off the flame.
  6. Serve hot.
